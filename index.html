About Osho

Osho was one of the most influential mystics of the 20th century whose revolutionary approach to meditation continues to inspire millions worldwide. Born as Rajneesh Chandra Mohan in 1931, he experienced spiritual awakening at the age of 21 and dedicated his life to helping others discover their inner truth.

Osho believed that meditation is not something separate from life but a quality you can bring to every activity. His teachings emphasized living fully and consciously, embracing both celebration and silence. He rejected dogmatic religions and encouraged individuals to question everything, discover their personal truth through direct experience, and break free from societal conditioning.

His unique contribution was creating dynamic and active meditation techniques specifically designed for contemporary people. Recognizing that modern individuals find it difficult to just sit still, he developed methods that allow expression and release of stress through movement, catharsis, and celebration before settling into silent awareness.

Osho - Enlightened Master

"Meditation is the awareness that 'I am not the mind.' When awareness goes deeper and deeper, slowly moments of silence, moments of pure space, moments of transparency arrive. In those still moments you will know who you are." - Osho

The Venue - Shri Tara Heritage Resort

Nestled in the serene hills of Sanchi, near the famous UNESCO World Heritage Site of Sanchi Stupa, the resort provides an ideal setting for meditation and spiritual practice.

The peaceful environment, surrounded by ancient Buddhist monuments, creates a sacred atmosphere perfect for inner exploration. Modern amenities combined with spiritual ambiance make it an ideal location for our meditation festival.
